Flora (C#)

Flora is a Free to Play PC game on Steam with 40K+ Downloads and a 70% positive review rating. I was the project lead for this game, doing most of the design work as well as a lot of programming, art, and project management.

  • Oversee engineering, plan code structure and weekly goals to meet deadlines, break down daily tasks and delegate tasks to team members.
  • Build some of the the core mechanics of the game, including the camera, player controller, and player abilities.
  • Extend features like input and time that are built into the Unity engine, provide teammates with easy-to-use interface that fits needs of the game.
  • Design gameplay mechanics and gameplay objectives.
