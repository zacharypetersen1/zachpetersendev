Dravenwatch (Blueprints)

My take on what Draven (from League of Legends) would be like if he were an Overwatch hero.

  • Design how Draven's abilities will transition from MOBA to FPS and implement them with Blueprints.
  • Recreate Overwatch's UI.
  • Create "Target Dummies" that behave like idle players.
  • Add sounds that mimic Overwatch's style and also provide important information to the player.
