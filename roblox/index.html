Roblox (C++, Lua)

Roblox is an online platform where kids can make and play games. Over 15 million games have been made for the platform and it has over 50 million monthly active users. I was a software engineering intern and I worked on the Xbox app.

  • Shipped toggle voice chat feature.
  • Designed and prototyped experimental in-game main menu screen.
  • Discovered and helped fix bug that broke the menu translations.
  • Created experimental demo that allowed users to synchronize their Roblox scene with Blender and render using the Cycles path tracing renderer.
